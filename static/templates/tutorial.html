< How It Works? />

Uploading

1. Upload your paper that you want to translate as a DOCX file

2. After submitting your paper, you will receive a unique code for the translation

This code is permanent and cannot be change, for each paper you will get a different code

Translate

1. Insert your code, where shown in the picture below.

Click "Get Started"

2. You will be given a paragraph to translate (see picture)

If you want you can download the original paper to understand the subtext of your paragraph.
Also you can see "Google translation" for you paragraph, do not rely on that to be a good translation
we are rely on you to translate the paragraph in the best why you can.

Some Tips

  • terms - write both translate and the non-translate meaning, put the non-translate in brackets
  • use bold, italic, and etc. to get better visual translate paper
  • translate more than 1 paragraph, you will speed up the translation!
